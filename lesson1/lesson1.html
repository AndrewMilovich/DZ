Early life

Keanu Charles Reeves was born in Beirut, Lebanon, on September 2, 1964, the son of Patricia (née Taylor), a costume designer and performer, and Samuel Nowlin Reeves Jr. His mother is English, originating from Essex.[8] His American father is from Hawaii, and is of Native Hawaiian, Chinese, English, Irish and Portuguese descent.[5][9][10] His grandmother is Chinese Hawaiian.[11] His mother was working in Beirut when she met his father,[12] who later abandoned his wife and family when Reeves was three years old.
